### Article
Getting to our Phang Nga white-water rafting base is easier than you might think. We handle all the logistics — you just need to tell us where you're staying, and we'll arrange the rest. Here's everything you need to know about transfers from the most popular Phuket beach areas.

## How Our Transfer System Works

When you book any of our [tour packages](/en#tours), round-trip hotel transfers are included in the price. That means our driver will pick you up in the morning and bring you straight to the rafting base in Phang Nga, then return you to your hotel at the end of the day.

The process is straightforward. Once you've chosen your tour — whether it's The Classic Rush, The Adventure Combo, or The Full Option — you tell us your hotel name and location during booking. We then assign you to the appropriate pickup zone based on where you're staying. On the morning of your tour, our air-conditioned minibus arrives at the designated time, and you're all set.

No rental cars, no worrying about finding the location, no navigating unfamiliar roads. Just relax and enjoy the ride.

## Pickup Times for Patong, Kata and Karon

If you're staying in Patong, Kata, or Karon, you're in luck — these are all free pickup zones with no additional transfer fees.

**Patong** is our largest pickup area, covering Sai Kor, Beach Road, and Tritrang. The earliest pickup starts at 07:45, so you'll want to be ready with your confirmation details the night before.

**Kata and Karon** share an earlier pickup window, with the first minibus departing at 07:30. If you're staying in Kathu, that same 07:30 pickup time applies. These southern beach areas are particularly convenient since the drive to Phang Nga takes roughly 90 minutes, meaning you'll arrive at the base by mid-morning and have plenty of time for your adventure.

It's worth noting that pickup times aren't exact — they're the earliest time our driver may arrive. We group guests heading to the same destination, so your actual pickup might vary by 10-15 minutes either way. Our team will confirm your exact pickup time the evening before your tour via WhatsApp, so keep your phone handy.

## What to Tell Us When Booking

To ensure a smooth pickup, we need a few specific details when you make your reservation. First, tell us your exact hotel name and address. "Somewhere near Patong Beach" won't work — we need the official hotel name so our driver knows exactly where to go.

Second, let us know if you're staying in a villa, apartment, or guesthouse rather than a named hotel. These can be trickier to locate, and a street name or landmark helps enormously.

Third, if your group is split across multiple hotels, let us know. We can usually arrange a central pickup point, or our driver can make multiple stops if the locations are close together.

Finally, mention any special circumstances — perhaps someone in your party has mobility issues, or you're travelling with very young children. This helps us prepare and ensure the right vehicle size and any necessary assistance.

You can reach us on WhatsApp at 66650102184 for any booking questions or to confirm your pickup details. We respond quickly and speak English fluently.

## Other Pickup Zones Worth Knowing

While Patong, Kata, and Karon cover the majority of our guests, we offer free transfers from most Phuket locations. Kamala, Surin, and Kalim enjoy free pickup from 08:15, while Phuket Town and Leam Hin guests are collected from 08:45. Bangtao and Laguna areas are picked up from 09:00, and Nai Yang, the Airport, and Mai Khao from 09:15.

A small number of areas carry a nominal fee. Naithon and Layan are THB 200, while Nai Harn, Rawai, Panwa, Ao Yon, and Siray Bay are THB 300. These areas are further from our base or require detours, so the fee reflects the additional travel time.

For Leam Krating, Ao Por, and Leam Yamu, we can arrange private transfers — just contact us directly for pricing since these require custom arrangements.

## What Happens If Your Hotel Isn't Listed?

If you don't see your area mentioned, don't worry. The zones above cover the vast majority of Phuket accommodation, but there are always exceptions. Simply message us on WhatsApp with your hotel name and location, and we'll let you know exactly what applies. We'll never leave you stranded — if we can't pick you up directly, we'll suggest the nearest convenient meeting point.

## FAQ

### How early do I need to be ready for pickup?
For Patong, Kata, and Karon, be ready by 07:30 at the latest. Our driver will arrive within that window, so have your bags packed and breakfast finished before then.

### Can I request a specific pickup time?
Pickup times are fixed by zone to ensure efficient routing. However, if you're travelling with young children or have specific needs, let us know and we'll see what we can arrange.

### What if my hotel is between two pickup zones?
We'll assign you to the closest zone based on your exact address. The system isn't perfect, but we always aim to minimise your wait time.

### Is the transfer definitely included in the tour price?
Yes. Every tour on our [What Do Phuket Adventure Tours Actually Cost in 2026?](/en/blog/what-phuket-adventure-tours-cost) page includes round-trip hotel transfer from all free pickup zones. You'll only pay extra if you're in a paid zone like Nai Harn or Naithon.
